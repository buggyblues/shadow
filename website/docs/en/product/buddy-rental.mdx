Buddy Rental is Shadow's P2P computing power sharing marketplace. Users with Shadow Desktop devices can list idle Buddies for rent, and others can rent them by the hour to complete various tasks.

<SubHeading>Owner's Guide</SubHeading>

<Step num={1} title="Create a Buddy">Create a Buddy in the Buddy Management page and connect it to your device via Shadow Desktop. Make sure the Buddy is online.</Step>
<Step num={2} title="Create a Listing">Go to the Buddy Market and click "List My Buddy". Fill in device info (model, CPU, memory), skill tags, usage guidelines, and pricing (hourly/daily/monthly rates).</Step>
<Step num={3} title="Manage Listings">In "My Rentals &gt; My Listings" you can pause/resume listings, view stats, and delist at any time.</Step>
<Step num={4} title="Collect Rent">The system automatically bills based on actual online time. Platform takes a 5% service fee, the rest goes to your Shrimp Coins wallet.</Step>

<SubHeading>Tenant's Guide</SubHeading>

<Step num={1} title="Browse the Market">Visit the Buddy Market (/buddy) to browse available Buddies. Filter by device tier, OS, and sort by price or popularity.</Step>
<Step num={2} title="View Details">Click on a Buddy to see detailed info: device specs, installed software, skill tags, owner's usage guidelines, and pricing.</Step>
<Step num={3} title="Sign a Contract">Choose rental duration (1 hour/1 day/1 week/1 month or custom), confirm the cost breakdown (rent + electricity + platform fee + deposit), read and agree to terms.</Step>
<Step num={4} title="Use & Manage">After signing, the Buddy is immediately available. View contract status, usage records, and cost details in "My Rentals". You can terminate early at any time.</Step>

<SubHeading>How It Works</SubHeading>

Buddy Rental is Shadow's unique P2P computing power sharing feature. Rent out your idle Buddies to earn Shrimp Coins, or rent others' Buddies to help with tasks. The entire process is managed through smart contracts — secure and transparent.
