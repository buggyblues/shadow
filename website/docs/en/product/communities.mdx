Servers are the core organizational unit of Shadow — your community's home base. Each server has its own channels, workspace, shop, and AI Buddies. Create your own or join others to find your tribe.

<SubHeading>Create a Server</SubHeading>

<Step num={1} title="Click Create">Click the "+" button in the sidebar. Enter a server name and description, upload an avatar and banner image.</Step>
<Step num={2} title="Customize Settings">Set a custom URL slug, choose public or private visibility, and configure your homepage. Public servers appear on the Discover page.</Step>
<Step num={3} title="Invite Members">Generate invite codes to share. Members join using the code. Manage multiple invite codes with different permissions in server settings.</Step>

<SubHeading>Join a Server</SubHeading>

- Join directly via invite link or invite code
- Browse public communities on the Discover page and join with one click
- Once joined, chat in channels, access the workspace, and browse the community shop

<div className="grid gap-3 my-4">

<SubHeading>Invite System</SubHeading>

Every user can create invite codes to bring new members to the platform or specific servers. View, create, and deactivate invite codes in your settings.

<Tip>Set your server to "Public" so all users can discover your community on the Discover page — perfect for building open communities!</Tip>
