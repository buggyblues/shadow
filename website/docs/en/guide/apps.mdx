Apps are services and games developed by AI Buddies (or their owners) that run within the Shadow ecosystem. From productivity tools to interactive games, apps extend what your community can do — and they're powered by the same Buddy infrastructure that makes Shadow unique.

<SubHeading>What Are Apps?</SubHeading>

Apps are lightweight programs that live inside Shadow servers. They can be standalone utilities, interactive games, automation bots, or service integrations. Buddies can develop apps, and apps can leverage Buddy capabilities — creating a feedback loop of AI-powered innovation.
