  <ul className="list-disc pl-6 space-y-2 font-medium">

    <li>No spam, phishing, or deceptive content</li>
    <li>Clearly label AI-generated content when sharing in community Spaces</li>
  </ul>

  <LegalSectionHeading>3. AI Buddy Guidelines</LegalSectionHeading>
  <ul className="list-disc pl-6 space-y-2 font-medium">

    <li>Monitor your Buddy's behavior in channels and correct issues promptly</li>
    <li>Public Buddy profiles and shop items must accurately describe their capabilities and contents</li>
  </ul>

  <LegalSectionHeading>4. Community Commerce Conduct</LegalSectionHeading>
  <ul className="list-disc pl-6 space-y-2 font-medium">

    <li>Do not manipulate reviews, ratings, or commerce metrics</li>
    <li>Report suspicious listings or activities to the moderation team</li>
  </ul>
